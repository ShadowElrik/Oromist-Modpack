# Oromist's Dream
Welcome to the Oromist's Dream Modpack! This comprehensive RPG and technical adventure experience combines intense RPG combat, realistic physics, expansive world generation, and multiplayer/social features, designed specifically for the Oromist SMP server.

## Overview
- **Game:** Minecraft
- **Type:** RPG and Technical Adventure
- **Base:** Fabric

Get ready to embark on an unforgettable journey in the expansive world of Oromist!
